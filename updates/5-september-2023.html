--- title: Introducing... PixelBuilds! ---

23 August 2023


Hey there everyone!

Today I am happy to announce something big both I and ExactExampl have been working on in the background.

PixelBuilds

PixelBuilds is a reworked Pixel-like feeling ROM with quality of life features!

Ongoing works are happening on the following devices:

  • Google Pixel 2 XL
  • Google Pixel 4
  • Google Pixel 4a

I intend to proceed with developing and shaping this ROM, whilst maintaining my previous device lineup at PixelExperience since my recent departure from the team.

Our aim isn't to clutter any daily usage, instead to focus on the essentials of using your Android device.

With the latest features from Pixel devices, merged directly into source, you'll be able to feel like a Pixel in no time!.
