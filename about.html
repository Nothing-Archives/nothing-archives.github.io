Nothing Index

About

Hello everyone!

Welcome to your go-to hub for everything Nothing and CMF. This website is a dedicated community-powered index that tracks all the official and community-shared updates for Nothing Phones and CMF devices. Whether you're looking for the latest firmware releases, changelogs, feature rollouts, or helpful support threads, you'll find it all organized here in one place. Built by enthusiasts, for enthusiasts, this platform aims to keep the Nothing community connected, informed, and up to speedâ€”without the noise.

Goals

  • Maintain a clean and organized index of every software update, patch, and changelog for Nothing and CMF phones.
  • Provide a platform for community members to share their experiences, tips, and tricks related to Nothing devices.
  • Encourage collaboration among developers, enthusiasts, and users to enhance the overall experience of Nothing phones.
  • Foster a sense of community by sharing news, events, and discussions related to Nothing and CMF.
  • Keep the index up-to-date with the latest information, ensuring that users have access to the most relevant content.
  • Help users stay informed with accurate timelines and details of firmware releases and feature rollouts.
  • Provide helpful resources, guides, and troubleshooting support to assist users at every step.
                 

Bio

James Gatt is an AOSP Engineer based in Malta.

He is currently undertaking Bsc. Computer Engineering in the University of Malta, whilst progressing his Android Engineering expertise in his free time.

James' journey started from a curiousity at a young age from which sparked interest in building AOSP, using the C programming language, Ubuntu and any related self-taught knowledge.

Throughout his journey, James embraced and contributed to the open-source community, by sharing his work on platforms like GitHub and GitLab, whilst also contributing patches and review on several Gerrit platforms.

Later in his Android career, James took his chances and applied as a conference speaker at major Android conferences to share his experience with AOSP and custom ROMs in events like Android Worldwide, droidcon and Google I/O Extended Malta.
